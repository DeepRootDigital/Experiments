San Diego, California

Clearly one of the world's best climates and an unparalleled quality of life encourages innovation and entrepreneurship. With 70 miles of coastline, 33 beaches, and an average daily temperature of 70.5 degrees Fahrenheit year-round, San Diego is not only a pleasant place to visit, but a rewarding place to live and work. Here you’ll see a wide range of lifestyle choices, from urban chic to rural peace. And there are few regions within the United States that offer as many residential options within a reasonable drive from major employment centers. With over 300 days of sunshine per year, San Diego’s climate is the ideal support for its’ diverse neighborhoods and lifestyles.
